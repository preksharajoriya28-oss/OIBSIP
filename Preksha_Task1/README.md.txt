📌 Task 1 – Cyber Crime Awareness Poster

 📖 Project Overview

This project is a *Cyber Crime Awareness Poster* designed using Canva to educate people about the increasing risks of cybercrime and encourage safe online practices. The poster uses a dark cyber-themed design with bold typography and high-contrast colors to immediately grab attention and effectively deliver the safety message.

The main objective of this design is to spread awareness about common online threats and remind users to stay alert while using the internet. It also highlights simple yet essential cybersecurity practices that everyone should follow in their daily digital life.


🎯 Objective

* Raise awareness about cybercrime.
* Encourage safe internet usage.
* Promote strong cybersecurity habits.
* Inform users about reporting cybercrime through the national helpline.

---

✨ Features

* Eye-catching cyber-themed visual.
* Dark red and black color palette representing cyber threats.
* Bold typography for maximum readability.
* Important cybersecurity tips included.
* Emergency cybercrime helpline (1930).
* Modern poster layout with balanced spacing.


🎨 Design Process

The poster was created in Canva by selecting a dark cyber-security themed background. Different typography styles were used to emphasize the main awareness message. Safety tips were arranged in a checklist format to make them easy to read, while contrasting colors such as red, white, and black were used to create urgency and visual impact.


 🛠️ Tools Used

* Canva
* Canva Elements
* Custom Typography
* Icons & Graphic Assets



🎨 Color Palette

* Black
* Red
* White

These colors symbolize danger, awareness, and seriousness while maintaining a professional cyber-security appearance.


🔤 Typography

Multiple bold fonts were used to create a strong visual hierarchy and ensure the important messages stand out clearly.


📌 Key Awareness Points

* Don't share OTPs.
* Don't click suspicious links.
* Don't trust unknown messages.
* Use strong passwords.
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
* Keep software updated.
* Report cybercrime at **1930**.

🚀 Outcome

This poster successfully communicates essential cybersecurity practices through a visually appealing and informative design. It aims to educate users and encourage responsible online behavior.
