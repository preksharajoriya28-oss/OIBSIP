📌 Task 3– Business Card Design (Kaira Jewellery Store)

📖 Project Overview

This project is a *Luxury Business Card Design created using Canva* for a premium jewellery brand named *Kaira Jewellery Store*. The design focuses on elegance, sophistication, and modern branding while maintaining a clean and professional appearance. The business card consists of both *front and back side*, ensuring that the brand identity and essential contact information are presented in a visually appealing manner.

The front side emphasizes the brand name with a luxurious background image showcasing premium jewellery, while the back side provides complete business details, social media information, and the range of jewellery collections offered by the brand.

🎯 Objective

* Create a premium and memorable business card.
* Strengthen the brand identity of a luxury jewellery store.
* Present business contact details in a professional manner.
* Maintain a clean, elegant, and visually attractive layout.
* Design a card suitable for networking, branding, and customer communication.

✨ Features

* Double-sided business card design.
* Luxury and minimalist aesthetic.
* High-quality jewellery lifestyle background.
* Elegant serif typography.
* Well-organized contact information.
* Social media and website details included.
* Collection highlights displayed on the back side.
* Professional spacing and balanced composition.


🎨 Design Process

The business card was designed using Canva by selecting a luxury jewellery-themed background that instantly communicates elegance and sophistication. The front side focuses on creating strong brand recognition by highlighting the company name using premium typography. Decorative sparkle elements were added to enhance the luxurious appearance.

The back side was designed to provide all essential business information in a structured layout. Contact details, website, email, social media handles, and store address were positioned on the left side for easy accessibility, while the jewellery collections and services were listed on the right side to showcase the brand's offerings. Proper alignment, spacing, and typography hierarchy were maintained to ensure readability without compromising the premium aesthetic.

🔤 Typography

The design combines modern serif fonts with clean sans-serif typography to create a refined and luxurious visual identity. Large uppercase lettering enhances brand visibility, while smaller fonts ensure clear presentation of business information.
